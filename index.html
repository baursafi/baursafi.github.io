Hello and Welcome!

My name is Baurjan Safi.

I am a data scientist and a financial professional. I just completed a course in Data Science Immersive at General Assembly. It is a 12-week course focused on statistics, linear algebra, coding in Python and its packages for machine learning algorithms. In the past I worked in corporate finance. I did financial analytics, evaluations, modeling, and cost analysis. The new set of skills, acquired at GA helped me find new interests like neural networks, machine learning algorithms, predictive analytics in corporate finance.

A Short Bio

I was born and raised in Kazakhstan, a country of vast steppes, cold winters and motherland of apples. Since the age of 14, I studied math and physics and later did my bachelor's in physics. Since the Soviet Union collapsed, I retrained to become an English-Russian interpreter and later did Master's in Business Administration. Ever since 1998, I worked in corporate finance and financial analysis. Now the time has come to restart a new career and I decided to return to where I started 30 years ago. Now I am a data scientist.
